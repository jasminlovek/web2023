About Me

Hi there! My name is Jasmin Keith and I am from Elizabethtown, Kentucky. I am a senior at the University of Kentucky where I study Information Communication Technology. I've always loved creating things. Not only that, but I love attention to detail! I was really eager to learn how to bring the designs in my head to life on a webpage. To me, it's a lot of fun. I think that that's what people should do for the rest of their lives if they can- find something that they love and find fun. Or, at least find some time to pursue that passion.
